About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to combat the stigma surrounding mental health and drug abuse among immigrant youth and parents. We strive to educate and empower immigrant parents with the knowledge and tools, drawing from our own experiences as immigrant parents in Canada, to successfully raise children within dual cultural environments

More about us

Our Values

We believe in approaching every individual with kindness,
understanding, and respect, recognizing the unique challenges
faced by immigrant families.

Our Goals

To empower and support immigrant families in Canada by advocating for mental health awareness and combating substance abuse stigma

Through education and community engagement, we aim to equip immigrant parents with the necessary tools and knowledge to navigate dual cultural environments successfully, fostering healthy, resilient, and thriving families."

OUR OBJECTIVES

Establish a supportive community network where immigrant families can access peer support, guidance, and resources to address parenting challenges and promote well-being.
